Frequently Asked Questions

Here is the list of the most asked question:

Why a fixed gear ?

There is several reasons to explain this choice :
  • Fixed gear is much more fun that geared bike !
  • I really like the overall look of the bike, it's very pure
  • In Paris there is no elevation so I don't need gear
  • I like to be fast

How light is it ?

Around 7.5kg ! The main factors of weight saving are :
  • The frame. Mine is in alumnium but carbone is even lighter
  • Wheelset, this one is a Miche Pistard wheelset (made for velodrome)
  • No brakes neither gears
