LEADER KAGERO

Story

At the beggining, I didn't planned to buy a new bike frame. I really liked my older frame but it had the seat tube seized... I tried everything to pull it out but nothing worked as wondered. During the second lockdown I was still trying to save the frame from my flat but one day I just asked someone what was the price of the Leader Kagero frameset. I liked the design and wanted to have an idea of the price, but after some hesitations and talks with the owner, I decided to buy this frameset in November.

The plan was to get almost all the components that were on my old frame, but when I get the frameset I thought it was too nice and deserved better components. So I ended up spending more money than expected, but building a really nice bike !

Specs

Here are all the components of the bike as it stands at the moment :

  • Frameset : Leader Kagero 2011
  • Wheelset : Miche Pistard WR
  • Seattube : Cinelli Pillar
  • Saddle : Specialized RBX
  • Crankset : BLB Track
  • Pedals : Shimano M540
  • Chain : Izumi Track Chain
  • Stem : Cinelli Ant
  • Handlebars : BMC 600mm widebar
